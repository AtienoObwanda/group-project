Air Fryer Kebabs
Ingredients:
  • Minced meat
  • Garlic/ginger paste
  • Salt
  • Ground cumin
  • Curry powder
  • Chili flakes
  • Lemon pepper salt
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
Method:
  1. Mix everything well and shape into oval kebab shapes.
  2. Place the kebabs in the air fryer and cook until well done
  3. Enjoy!
